ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Farha Saad. I live in Al-Mazra'a Al-Sharqiya and I am a graduate in Computer Engineering from Birzeit University. I am 23 years old and passionate about enhancing my skills in programming and modern technology. I enjoy exploring innovative tech solutions, and I am particularly interested in learning more about artificial intelligence. My future goal is to work in a global tech company and contribute to the development of smart systems. Additionally, I aspire to create my own project focused on interactive educational applications for children.

MY TOWN

Al-Mazra'a Al-Sharqiya (Arabic: المزرعة الشرقيّة) is a Palestinian town in the Ramallah and al-Bireh Governorate, located northeast of Ramallah in the northern West Bank. According to the Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics (PCBS), the town had a population of 4,063 inhabitants in 2017.
